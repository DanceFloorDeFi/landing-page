Overview

Any NFT can be wrapped into a fungible single type of ERC-20 token, a Wrapped Floor-price NFT ($FLOOR). These $FLOOR tokens can be traded on any DEX, used as liquidity in lending protocols, or held as an index or fractional shares of the NFTs locked in the protocol. The $FLOOR tokens are backed 1:1 in value by the NFTs locked into Dance Floor.

The holders of the $DANCE governance token vote on which types of NFTs can be used in the protocol.

Get started:

Use our NFT/FLOOR liquidity pool to instantly sell your NFTs, swap between ones you own and ones in our pool, or redeem FLOOR tokens.
